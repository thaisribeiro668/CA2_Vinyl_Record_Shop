Our Story

Born from a
love of the groove.

Groove & Grain started as a crate-digging obsession and grew into Dublin's most carefully curated vinyl destination. Every record we stock has been listened to, loved, and hand-picked.

We believe vinyl is
not a format — it's a feeling.

It started in 2019 with a handful of Jazz records, a secondhand turntable, and far too much enthusiasm. What began as a side project quickly became something we couldn't walk away from.

Today, Groove & Grain is a proper shop — but we've kept the soul of that first collection: obsessive curation, honest pricing, and a genuine love for the music. We travel to record fairs across Europe, work directly with estates and labels, and spend more time than we probably should hunting down that one pressing you can't find anywhere else.

Whether you're a seasoned collector or just getting your first turntable set up, you'll find something here worth playing loud.

What we stand for

Listened, not just listed

Every record we carry has been played. We don't stock what we haven't heard. Our curation comes from ears, not algorithms.

Community first

We host listening sessions, DJ nights, and open crate events. The shop is a gathering place as much as a store.

Honest grading

We grade conservatively and describe accurately. No surprises when you open the sleeve — what you read is what you get.
